19-year-old Wenswood girl reported missing
She was last seen at home yesterday afternoon. Authorities request that anyone with information come forth.

By Jennifer Johnson, Emergency Reporter
May 12, 2021

A 19-year-old girl named Eliza Wilson was reported missing on Wednesday afternoon according to the Wenswood police. The police were informed by her father at about 11 a.m. after she had not come home in at least 16 hours.

”Eliza has always been such a good girl. She’s quiet and reserved. She never even parties. She told her mom she was going out to do a group study for an exam. It makes no sense that she didn’t come home for the night,” said Eliza’s father. ”If something happened, I’m confident that she would have given us a call. Something is very wrong here.”

”We’ve been thinking of the worst possibilities, especially when it’s so chaotic and dangerous out there,” Eliza’s mother added. “I never should have let her out in the evening.”

Wenswood police have called for submission of any evidence and footage related to Eliza’s disappearance. This story is still in development.
